Topeka

Topeka is the capitol city in the state of Kansas.

Skyline of the city of Topeka

I was surprised to find that Topeka has a significant place in the history of racial equality in America. The city was originally founded as an anti-slavery town, and is famous for the landmark case of Brown v Board of Education which ended racial segregation in schools.
