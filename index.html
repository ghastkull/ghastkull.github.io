The State of Kansas

This website will be about the U.S. State of Kansas. I chose this state for this task because I am considering moving there, specifically somewhere in the Kansas City area. I decided to use this task as a way to do some research about the state and the KC area, as such I included a few additional cities/towns near, or within, the Kansas City metro region.

State Capitol of Kansas

The capitol of Kansas is Topeka. The state flag, shown in the above navigation menu, contains a Latin phrase "Ad astra per aspera" which means "To the stars through difficulties". As of the 2020 census, the population of Kansas is 2,940,865. Kansas, and the cities I chose to focus on in the east border of the state, have prominent history in the Civil War era.
