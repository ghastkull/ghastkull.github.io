Kansas City

"Kansas City" is actually a major metropolitan area that is shared between the states of Kansas and Missouri.

Cityscape of Kansas City, Kansas

Kansas City, KS is locally abbreviated to "KCK" to distinguish it from Kansas City, MO ("KCMO"). Kansas City resides on the border between the states of Kansas and Missouri. This city was actually incorporated a second time in 1886 when five original municipalities were consolidated. Kansas City is one of the U.S.'s top 100 largest cities.
